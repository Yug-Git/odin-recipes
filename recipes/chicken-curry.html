Chicken Curry

Description

Chicken curry is a flavorful dish made by simmering chicken in a spiced onion-tomato gravy. It’s rich, aromatic, and commonly eaten with rice or roti.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken (bone-in or boneless)
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • ½ cup yogurt (optional)
  • Spices: 1 tsp turmeric, 1½ tsp cumin, 1½ tsp coriander, 1 tsp garam masala, 1 tsp chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish
  • 3 tbsp oil

    • Steps

    1. Sauté Aromatics

      2. Heat oil, fry onions until golden. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until fragrant.

    2. Add Spices & Tomatoes

      3. Stir in turmeric, cumin, coriander, chili powder. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.

    3. Cook Chicken

      4. Add chicken, mix well, cook until sealed. Add yogurt (optional), cover, and simmer.

    4. Add Water

      5. Pour in ½–1 cup water for gravy. Simmer until chicken is tender.

    5. Finish

      6. Sprinkle garam masala, garnish with fresh coriander, and serve hot.
