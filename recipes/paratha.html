Paratha

Description

Paratha is a flaky, pan-fried Indian flatbread made with whole wheat flour. It can be plain or stuffed with fillings like potatoes, paneer, or vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¾ cup water (adjust as needed)
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee (for dough)
  • Ghee or oil (for frying)

Steps

  1. Make Dough

    2. Mix flour, salt, and water to form a soft dough. Rest for 20 minutes.

  2. Roll

    3. Divide into balls, roll each into a circle, fold and roll again for layers.

  3. Heat Pan

    4. Preheat a tawa or skillet on medium heat.

  4. Cook

    5. Place paratha on hot tawa, cook each side until brown spots appear.

  5. Fry

    6. Brush with ghee or oil, cook both sides until crisp and golden. Serve hot.
